About Castifi


Castifi is evolving the way the entertainment industry works. By seamlessly connecting actors to production companies through our app, we make casting more accessible which opens up possibilities for film/TV productions while creating opportunities for actors. Castifi's simple approach continues to bring people and their favorite shows and movies closer.
