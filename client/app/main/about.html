About Castifi


Castifi is evolving the way the entertainment industry works. Our app seamlessly connects actors to production companies. We make casting more accessible by opening up possibilities for film and TV productions while creating opportunities for actors. Castifi's simple approach brings people closer to their favorite shows and movies.
